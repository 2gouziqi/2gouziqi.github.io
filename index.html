About Me

Hello, I'm Ziqi Zhang, a final-year Master's student at ETH Zürich, specializing in Robotics, System and Control. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Aerospace Engineering from TU Delft. Currently, I'm conducting my Master's thesis at the Autonomous Systems Lab led by Prof. Dr. Roland Siegwart. I'm passionate about 3D vision and its applications in robotics and science research.

Research Interests

  • Computer Vision and 3D Reconstruction Techniques
  • Robotic Perception, SLAM, and Autonomous Navigation
  • Application of Vision Technologies in Aerospace Engineering
